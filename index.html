How the Game Works

The core philosophy of Endless Sky Runner is "easy to learn, hard to master." You play as an adventurer equipped with a jetpack flying through a sprawling sci-fi world. The core mechanic is simple:

  • Hold to Fly: Tap, click, or hold the Spacebar to ignite your jetpack and defy gravity.
  • Release to Fall: Let go to allow gravity to pull you smoothly back towards the ground.
  • Endless Progression: The screen scrolls continuously to the right. The farther you go, the higher your score.
  • Obstacles & Enemies: Dodge incoming hazards like electric zappers, laser beams, and homing missiles.
  • Collectibles: Gather coins to buy skins and grab power-ups in-run (Shields, Magnets, Speed Boosts, UI heart packs).

Technology Used

Built completely lightweight and natively without heavy game engines like Unity or Godot. The game achieves 60 FPS entirely in the browser.

HTML5 Canvas API Vanilla JS (ES6) CSS3 & HTML Web Audio API Google Gemini Claude Sonnet
  • HTML5 Canvas API: Renders all graphics, players, enemies, and backgrounds with dynamic scaling for responsive screens.
  • Vanilla JavaScript (ES6): Powers the game engine, delta-time physics, state management, and AABB collision detection.
  • CSS3 & HTML: Drives the sleek, cyber-HUD UI utilizing modern fonts (Outfit/Share Tech Mono).
  • Web Audio API: Mathematically synthesizes all sound effects on the fly—zero external MP3 files needed.

Hostile Drones

Beyond visual skins, the endless skies are populated by unique flying mechanical enemies:

Spinning Robot

Hovers erratically in mid-air and shoots standard bullets to the left with an unpredictable spread.

The Standard

Hovers stably at mid-height and fires a very fast, highly predictable straight laser beam.

Attack Chopper

Flies high near the ceiling and shoots distinct, downward-angled heavy fireballs.

Homing Missile

A high-speed tracking projectile marked by a warning alert. Weave vertically to dodge it!

Boss Encounters

Epic milestone battles that test your mastery of flight. All bosses flash a ⚠️ DODGE! telegraph right before they unleash hell.

Sentinel Turret 2,000m

A large automated defense platform that sweeps aggressively up and down, firing dual salvos of plasma bursts.

Warden Mech 4,000m

A massive bipedal walker. Fires rows of shells along the top and bottom, plus homing fireballs.

Overlord Gunship 6,000m & 12,000m

A high-tech ship alternating between "UP waves" and "DOWN waves" of missiles, forcing constant weaving.

The Annihilator 19,000m

The brutal Final Boss. Performs huge vertical sweeps and fires 3 massive energy waves in a fan pattern.
